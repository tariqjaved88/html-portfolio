The Best Movies According to Tariq

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time

  1. Gladiator

    Gladiator" is an epic historical drama film directed by Ridley Scott and released in the year 2000.
    Set in ancient Rome, the movie showcases the story of Maximus Decimus Meridius, a respected Roman general played by Russell Crowe.
    After the murder of his family and the betrayal of Emperor Commodus, portrayed by Joaquin Phoenix, Maximus is forced into slavery and becomes a gladiator.

  2. King Kong

    King Kong" is a timeless adventure film that has captivated audiences since its original release in 1933.
    Directed by Merian C. Cooper and Ernest B. Schoedsack, the movie tells the story of an ambitious filmmaker,
    Carl Denham, who ventures to the uncharted Skull Island in search of a legendary giant ape known as King Kong.

  3. Titanic

    Released in 1997, "Titanic" is a sweeping epic romance film directed by James Cameron.
    Set against the backdrop of the ill-fated RMS Titanic, the movie tells the tragic yet captivating love story of Jack Dawson,
    played by Leonardo DiCaprio, and Rose DeWitt Bukater, portrayed by Kate Winslet.
